The Sattvik Food Experience For All

ZEROTABLES is a Bangalore-based culinary initiative rooted in the ethos of purity, health, and tradition. At its core, ZEROTABLES believes that food is not just nourishment but a sacred expression of culture and wellness. Founded with a mission to deliver wholesome vegetarian meals, ZEROTABLES has established itself as a trusted name in sattvik cuisine — meals prepared without onion, garlic, or any ingredients that disturb the harmony of body and mind.

What We Do

Sattvik Meals

We specialize in preparing pure Sattvik meals, free from onion and garlic, designed to nourish your body and promote peace, clarity, and mental balance.

A Peaceful Lifestyle

Our food is rooted in ancient traditions, crafted to support a sattvic lifestyle that fosters calmness, mindfulness, and harmony through every thoughtfully prepared dish.

Spiritual Clarity

By eliminating onion and garlic, our meals support spiritual clarity and inner peace, ideal for meditation, wellness, and those seeking holistic nourishment through food.

Who We Are

Our Vision

To be a trusted destination for wholesome, Sattvik vegetarian meals that nourish the body, calm the mind, and inspire a lifestyle rooted in purity, balance, and mindful living.

Our Expertise

Backed by 40 years of corporate experience, our team deeply understands evolving urban tastes, aligning culinary expertise with customer needs to craft meals that consistently delight and satisfy modern lifestyles..

Our Values

Rooted in purity, authenticity, and sustainability, we prioritize customer delight, community welfare, compassion, mindfulness, and ethical cooking—nurturing well-being through every thoughtfully prepared, wholesome and spiritually uplifting Sattvik meal.
